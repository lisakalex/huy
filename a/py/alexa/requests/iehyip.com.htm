What Are Some of The Ways That Tech Has Improved Our Lives?

January 23, 2023
For anyone that has been born within the last thirty or so years, a life without technology may seem unimaginable to you. However, it hasn’t been that long since technology was introduced to the world and since its initial introduction, it has evolved and changed in ways we could never imagine. Here are some ways tech has improved our lives.

Made Life More Convenient For People With Disabilities

Technology has made life more convenient for people with disabilities by providing them with a wide range of accessible tools and services. For example, the availability of voice recognition software, text-to-speech conversion, and screen readers make it easier for people with visual impairments to access digital content, do online research, and communicate with others. Additionally, assistive technologies, such as wheelchairs, prosthetics, and hearing aids, help people with physical disabilities to better navigate the world. Furthermore, online shopping and delivery services, as well as ride-sharing services, provide people with disabilities with more options for transportation, employment, and entertainment.

Created More Work Opportunities

Tech has created more work opportunities for people from all walks of life in numerous ways. It has enabled people to work from home, given access to more freelance job opportunities, and allowed for online businesses to emerge and thrive. People can now easily set up an online store, marketplace, or blog to generate income, and tech has enabled the growth of the gig economy and remote work. Additionally, tech has enabled more efficient collaboration between teams and coworkers, making it easier to manage projects and complete tasks. Not everyone is able to go to work everyday and technology has made working from home easier than ever.

Added Convenience to Our Lives

As humans, we want everything that we do to be as convenient as humanly possible. Technology has improved the convenience of just about everything that we do and it has been an absolute life changer. For example, if you are someone with a busy schedule, you do not have to worry about heading to the shops to pick up food and you can have everything delivered to you in a matter of minutes. This means that as humans, we don’t have to feel so stressed all of the time.

Improved How We Treat Sick People

Another excellent innovation that has come with technology is changing the way that we are able to treat sick people. We can now complete complex surgeries that the human hand would never be capable of and that is all thanks to the innovations that we have experienced in technology. As well as improved surgeries, the way that we are able to get medication to sick people has also improved substantially. If you suffer from chronic pain and sickness, you can now use cbdmagic to improve your life and this can be delivered straight to your home, which is good if you have accessibility issues.

Transformed The Entertainment Industry

Another way that technology has changed our lives is when it comes to the entertainment industry. Before the introduction of technology, entertainment was limited to books, theater and other live entertainment. Since the introduction of technology, entertainment has evolved substantially and you can now play video games, enjoy VR and head out to see amazing entertainment. People have differing opinions over whether or not this is a good thing, as many people believe that increased entertainment options have made people really lazy, but if you think about all of the people who cannot get out and experience new things, these forms of entertainment are really important.

Improved Socialization

Before the introduction of technology, the people you could socialize with would be those in your local area and those who you could send a letter to. Thanks to technology and the creation of the internet, this has all changed. You can now socialize with people from all over the world in a matter of seconds. You can also use your mobile phone to ring people on the go, which is something nobody ever imagined to one day be possible. Having this technology was also very useful during the lockdown period.

Higher Security Standards

If you think back to the past and imagine all of the stories about people famously robbing banks or even trains, you tend to ask yourself, how did they do it? Well in today’s world, this is not something that would have been possible and that is all thanks to the help of technology. We now have much higher security standards thanks to technology, including CCTV and electronic locks to ensure that everyone is safe. This makes it much harder for people to commit crimes and it also means that life is a lot safer for everyone because of this.

Improved Police Investigation

There are a lot of crimes that we read about in history and many of us question how the criminal managed to get away with it. This is because the police did not have the technology that they have access to today. There were no fingerprint cross-referencing machines, there was no immediate blood testing and so it was easy for people to get away with crimes. Of course, this is no longer the case and thanks to technology, it is now easier than ever for police to solve crimes. Crime rates have become lower in the last few decades and the introduction of technology is to thank.

What is a VPS and Why Do You Need It?

December 28, 2022December 29, 2022

A VPS or a Virtual Private Server is a service that can be used to quickly share and store resources across various platforms. The data is stored on a physical server, with access shared among the users. This method of data sharing offers a lot more customizability to users’ preferences over more traditional shared hosting. Many business owners have begun moving into this form of data sharing for their sensitive documents, however, the benefits of this service can be applied to anyone with data-sharing needs. The possibilities for utilizing a VPS for Windows or Linux are endless.

Privacy

Using a VPS provides a much stronger quality of privacy and security for your sensitive material. The data is stored on a physical server, meaning that nothing is online to be mined for. VPS environments are all isolated from each other, with the only access being for the owner. Compared to a shared hosting service, which is vulnerable to a denial of service attack, your data and services are not susceptible to sharing the same vulnerability of other resources on a shared service. With a VPS you are also provided with root access to your environment, allowing you full customizability to each doorway into your network and data. You are in control of every aspect of your VPS, allowing you to manually increase your security services inside the network. VPS services are also backed up daily as part of some VPS services, however, backing up your server is incredibly easy and data loss is incredibly rare.

Fast and Reliable

VPS services are some of the fastest ways to store multiple resources and data while also maintaining fast boot-up times and access to the hosted data. You allocate your own bandwidth to the VPS, meaning you decide how fast your VPS will operate. Compared to a shared hosting service, you could be beholden to another customer’s immense bandwidth use. Your data shared on a VPS is all stored and controlled up to your standards, if you are needing to free up bandwidth for another reason, then you can do so and return the bandwidth back whenever you choose.

In addition to controlling how much bandwidth is allocated to your VPS, you are also in control of the storage that the VPS contains. If you are in need of more, then contacting your provider is easy and you can increase or decrease up to your own specifications. With the VPS being stored in a physical location with remote access, you are able to access your data and resources anywhere in the world. If your company is away from the office for any reason, you can connect to your VPS to gain access to any data you might need for remote work.

Hosting Resources

An average VPS can host up to 5-10 websites; a large number of customer files and data; in addition to hosting web servers and emails. This versatility in storage can be used in a number of different ways, including the creation of virtual workstations for your employees when working at home. Hosting your resources on a VPS allows for a quick and smooth workflow with all employees, with no employee being beholden to their own software or bandwidth. However, consider only keeping mission-critical resources on the VPS, and use all storage wisely with your VPS to ensure maximum efficiency. Hosting a high-traffic website on a VPS will provide instant benefits due to the heavy load of the website being put solely on the VPS. With a VPS service dedicated to only the data on its server, no aspect of your website is held back by other software.

Conclusion

There are plenty of reasons to utilize a VPS for your business, however, consider exactly what you will need the VPS for. Consider the amount of storage you will need, what you are aiming to get out of the VPS, and how much bandwidth you are able to allocate to the VPS. Take into account how much traffic you are expecting to see with the resources you share on your server. If you are expecting high traffic consider upgrading to more bandwidth allocation or looking into a dedicated server.

How to Make Remote Working Work For Your Business

October 12, 2022November 6, 2022

Over the last couple of years, something that has become increasingly more popular is working from home. A big reason why this is the case is because of Covid. When Covid hit, we were unable to head into the office and when we realized that covid would last a lot longer than we thought, we had to put some sort of plan into place.

The most reasonable and sensible plan that businesses put into place was allowing workers to work from home. At first, people were not very comfortable with working from home, but once people adjusted it became much easier and people started to prefer working from home. In fact, a lot of people started to prefer working from home and many people left their jobs in order to pursue a career that allowed them to work from home, even after the pandemic.

If you are a business owner, you may have also considered allowing your employees work from home. This allows you to save money on paying for an office and it ensures that your workers will be more comfortable. However, something that a lot of business owners worry about is how working from home affects productivity and whether or not it will work for your business.

We believe that essentially any business is able to thrive with remote working and here are some of the ways that you can make it work for your business.

Time Tracking

One of the biggest worries that employers have is that when their employees work from home, they do not do as much work as they should be doing. You have two options, you can either trust that they will be doing enough work or you can use a time card calculator. This will allow your workers to input what work they are doing and how long they have been doing it for. This ensures that you will know what they are doing at all times and it will give you comfort knowing that they are on task.

Define Your Goals

If you want your business to thrive while your employees work remotely, you need to take the time to define your goals. What are your overall objectives? What do you hope to achieve by having your employees work from home? Once you have a clear understanding of your goals, you can develop a plan to help you achieve them. By setting clear goals and creating a plan to achieve them, you can make remote working work for your business. This will also make what they need to do clearer for your workers.

Give Thorough Training

If you want your remote working arrangement to be successful, it is important to give your employees thorough training. This includes training on how to use the communication and collaboration tools that you will be using, as well as any other tools that they will need to do their job. You should also provide training on how to stay productive and organized when working from home. By giving your employees the tools and training they need to be successful, you can set your business up for success.

Evaluate Progress

When you are trying remote working for the first time, a lot of it really depends on trust and trial and error. You should allow your workers to work remotely for around three months and then you can do a full evaluation of the success that you have achieved. If you take the time to sit back and take a real look at the progress that has been made over the last few months, you will be able to properly decide whether or not you would like to continue with the working from home process.

Host Regular Zoom Meetings

If you’re running a business, it’s important to keep in touch with your employees, even if they’re working remotely. A great way to do this is to host regular Zoom meetings. This way, you can check in with everyone and see how they’re doing, as well as answer any questions they may have. Additionally, you can use Zoom meetings to brainstorm ideas and stay up-to-date on what’s going on with your business.

Listen to Feedback

In order to make remote working work for your business, it is important to listen to feedback from your employees. This will help you to identify any issues that they may be having and address them accordingly. Additionally, by listening to feedback, you can learn about any potential improvements that could be made to the remote working setup. You have to remember that working remotely isn’t only new for you, it is also new for your workers and so there may be some ways that you can help them adjust to this new way of working that they may not be familiar with.

Are 3D Printed Houses Fire Proof?

July 5, 2022July 5, 2022

You can’t always rely on naturally fire-resistant materials when building a home. You sometimes need to make your own home using prefabricated fireproof materials. A 3D printed house is undoubtedly one of the most innovative ideas that have come our way in recent history.

Those who are designing these buildings believe they will provide us with a better alternative to traditional architecture and construction materials and create opportunities for sustainable living. Some people have started questioning whether 3D-printed houses are fireproof or combustible.

3D Printed Houses

3D printed houses are an emerging trend in architectural design, and they have become more affordable over time. At first glance, 3D-printed houses may not seem very fireproof. However, there are several ways that you can build these homes to ensure they are safe in case of an emergency or other event.

The materials used to build a 3D-printed house differ from those used for traditional homes. Traditional houses are made up of wood, bricks, and cement. The 3D printed house uses plastic. Plastic is an excellent insulator that allows heat to pass through but not burn through. The plastic will also not be affected by water or humidity like other materials.

To give you a tip, the workmanship and design of any house can be an advantage to the insurance company. The crafting and composition of each component must meet building standards. Maybe this is why your claim for house fire insurance was denied since you hired an underqualified contractor to craft and install your house. Worry no more; the new architectural design can keep you and your family safe.

What Makes 3D Printed Houses Fireproof?

3D printed houses are made using additive manufacturing, which means that rather than making the entire structure from scratch, they are built up layer by layer. As a result, it is possible to create incredibly detailed models of any design imaginable. It makes them ideal for architects and designers who want to show off their work in a way that’s impossible with traditional construction methods. Here are other factors that make 3D Printed houses fireproof.

Adoption of Lavacrete

Lavacrete is a cement-based product used to make houses and other structures fireproof. The idea behind this material is to create a barrier between the outer layers of the house and the fuel source. This creates an insulation effect that reduces the heat traveling into the house, thereby reducing the chances of it catching fire.

Once you have inserted some insulation into your houses, such as drywall or fiberglass batting, whatever you do next should be done with caution. If you were to try installing drywall in your home without first inserting some insulation, it would result in more heat being transferred through your walls than needed.

Default Configuration of Concrete

The default concrete configuration in most houses is a 1:1:2 mix ratio (1 part cement, 1 part sand, 2 parts water). It means that there are three layers. The first layer consists of concrete and provides structural integrity.

Adoption Of Fiberglass Reinforcement As An Alternative For Steel

The best options for building a fireproof house are fiberglass and concrete. These materials are powerful enough to withstand fire heat yet lightweight enough not to cause structural damage when they burn. In addition, fiberglass is less prone than steel or concrete to cracking or melting when exposed to heat during a fire.

Wrapping Up

Many complex factors need to be considered for 3D-printed houses to be deemed entirely fireproof. The science behind the material is solid, and with the proper precautions put into place. While 3D printed houses can be fire-resistant, they still require much protection to be completely resistant to fire and soaring temperatures.

Why Minecraft is a Prime Example of How Tech can Inspire People

August 30, 2021

At all points of life, we are always looking for ways in which we can be inspired from random sources. There are many walks of life where we can gain random inspiration and when we get it, it can completely change our outlooks on life.

In recent years, tech has become a very important part of everyone’s lives. The world has gone almost completely digital, which means that everything that was once done manually can now be done via tech. The same can be said about inspiration as tech has been inspiring a generation for years now.

Many people think it is something for something material such as tech to be able to inspire people, but there are many examples of this simply not being the case. One of the best examples of this being the open-world game Minecraft. This game has been around for the best part of the last two decades and during this time it has inspired an entire generation of children and adults that have grown up playing this game. If you are someone that doesn’t have much experience with this game, then you may fail to see how the game could have inspired so many people. Here are just some of the many ways that Minecraft inspired an entire generation of people.

Architecture

Reports show that in the last ten years there has been a drastic increase in people choosing to study architecture at a higher education level. Many people believe that Minecraft is partly responsible for this. One of the main features of the game is being able to use the material to build just about anything you want. In fact, there is a creative mode that is specifically dedicated to those who love the building aspect of the game and it gets rid of any of the limits that come with the original game mode, allowing the player to access any material to be able to build with.

If you have a quick look on google, you will be able to see many examples of what people have managed to make on the game and the creativity of a lot of people’s creations will really surprise you. Players have made whole towns with detailed buildings throughout which seem like they would be almost impossible to make.

Many people that spent time testing out their building ability on Minecraft were then inspired to test their skills in real life and venture into the world of architecture, where they could really see what their imagination could produce in real life. Many believe that Minecraft is actually to thank for some of the greatest architectural minds of the 21st century and as the features of the game continue to improve, we believe that there will only be more people inspired to pursue a career in the world of architecture.

 

Computing

As well as inspiring a generation of architects, it is also believed that Minecraft inspired a generation of computer programmers. Though computing isn’t one of the main aspects of the game when you play and own online Minecraft servers there can be some programming involved in order to successfully run a Minecraft server unless you just connect to a server that is available on the Minecraft server list. For those of us that wanted to host private servers for our friends, we were forced to pick up some programming knowledge from quite a young age.

For some people picked up some programming knowledge, developed an interest in the world of programming, and decided to further develop their knowledge through their own research and pursuit of further education. Without Minecraft being a part of everyone’s life, we may not have the programmers that we have today which means we may not have some of the games that we love and we may not have some of the applications that we all depend on every day.

 

Animal Lovers

Something that is not very commonly known about Minecraft is that it has a vast list of animals that you can find in the game. Though the game can not be one hundred percent accurate all of the time, the game does try to stick to some accuracies about the animals that they have within the game.

The best examples of where they try to stay accurate are through the biomes that you can find the animals in and what the animals in the game eat. In recent years, they have become more specific with the features that the animals have. When they introduced bees into the game, they added the feature that when you injure the bees they will fight back and hurt your character. This was done in order to teach people how to properly interact with insects in real life and this is actually very valuable information.

They more recently added axolotls to the game and they became one of the most accurate depictions of the animals yet and they can only be very rarely found and within dark bodies of water, which is true to real life as axolotls can only be found in the wild in one specific lake in Mexico.

The introduction of animals to the game inspired a lot of people to further their knowledge of animals and many Minecraft users have actually gone into animal-based careers and have searched for ways to help look after some animals that are going extinct.
