Exclusive to users in Australia
Jan 19, 2023

Australian users, the UFC is returning to Perth and we’re giving away a pair of tickets to a lucky winner, along with a travel subsidy of AU$750! To participate in the lucky draw, simply spend with Crypto.com Pay and accumulate a value of at least AU$150 during the campaign period. Crypto.com Pay is accepted at all OTR stores, as well as at many more global and local merchants.

The UFC 284 Lightweight Championship will take place at the RAC Arena in Perth, Australia on 12 February 2023, and is headlined by Islam Makhachev and the current UFC Featherweight Champion – Alexander Volkanovski. This is the eighth time in UFC history that champions from different divisions are fighting for the same title, so don’t miss out on witnessing this exciting fight!

Campaign Period:  20 January 2023, 00:00 AEDT to 5 February 2023, 23:59 AEDT
